Why watchlist data can differ by user
TV — If you have your own Sonarr connection, TV shows the full upcoming Sonarr list. Without Sonarr, TV is built from your Jellyfin watch history, so it only shows upcoming episodes for shows you appear to watch.
Movies — If you have your own Radarr connection, Movies shows the full upcoming Radarr list. Without Radarr, Movies falls back to your Seerr requests, so the list may be smaller.
Calendar — Calendar combines the TV and Movie sources available to your account.
